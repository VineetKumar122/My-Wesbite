Limited Power of Attorney (LPA): Empowering Your Legal Affairs A Limited Power of Attorney (LPA) is a legal document that grants a designated individual, known as the "agent" or "attorney-in-fact," the authority to make specific decisions and take actions on behalf of the person creating the LPA, known as the "principal." Unlike a General Power of Attorney, which grants broad authority over all legal and financial matters, an LPA is tailored to cover only specific tasks or transactions.
